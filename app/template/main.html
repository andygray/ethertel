  1. "Make a Call" on the EtherTel payphone.
    Select a destination, type the number to call, and the estimated mins of the call (over estimate as we refund the unused seconds!)
  2. Once you have a quote, send the ether (in wei) to create a call transaction in the exchange...
  3. When you have finished chatting the provider will report the number of seconds used and you will be refunded (and the provider gets their ether for the call)
