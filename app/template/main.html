  1. "Make a Call" on the EtherTel payphone
  2. Select a destination, type the number to call, and the estimated mins of the call
  3. Once you have a quote, send the ether (in wei)
  4. On completion get the exchange will refund your unused ether (and pay the provider!)

For this demo we have signed you in with a test account with plenty of ether!
